Here are three of my favorite hobbies:

Photography

I love capturing beautiful moments and experimenting with lighting and composition. It forces me to be quick, but present. I’ve learned that moments don’t wait. Sometimes it’s just a second, a look, a shadow—and if I hesitate, it’s gone. It’s taught me to pay attention in a sharper way, to really be there instead of drifting

Reading

Diving into books allows me to explore new worlds and ideas beyond reality. When I read, it slows me down. I don’t just follow stories anymore, I start noticing the small things,the pauses, the emotions underneath what’s being said. It kind of spills into real life too. I’ll be sitting somewhere busy, and instead of just seeing people, I start imagining their stories, their tensions, their quiet moments.

Traveling

Exploring new places and cultures enriches my perspective on life. Traveling has taught me to be adaptable and open-minded. It’s like stepping into a new story every time, and it reminds me that there’s so much more out there than what I know. It’s made me more curious about people and their lives, and it’s given me a deeper appreciation for the diversity of the world. But it also humbles me, reminds me that my perspective is just one small piece of a much bigger picture.
